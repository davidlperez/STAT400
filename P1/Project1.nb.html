The outputs given by analyzing the data of account ownership dependent on education level seems to have a similar change in 2017. A number of groups analyzed change direction during this year, but there isnt much correlation shown relating to education level, as those with primary education in some countries exhibit tendencies that those in other countries with higher than a secondary education also show. The one major outlier is China with a primary education or less, as it stays around 10-20% lower than the rest of the groups in this analysis. Similar to the gender study, omitting 2017 would not allude to any conclusions about the the percentage of account ownership across different countries. However, if one country and education combination were to be focused on, it is possible a constant increase or decrease can be inferred.
